# MindBridge

MindBridge is a cross-platform **Neural Operating System** designed to unify your intelligence across all major AI platforms. It acts as a persistent bridge between ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and your local environment, ensuring that your context, identity, and memory are never lost.

## Core Mission

The current AI landscape is fragmented. Your history in ChatGPT doesn't know about your research in Claude. MindBridge solves this by:

1.  **Unifying Identity**: Carrying your "Neural Persona" across platforms.
2.  **Persistent Memory**: Using ChromaDB to store and retrieve long-term context.
3.  **Real-time Bridging**: Passing context between tabs instantly.

## Key Features

- **Neural Identity Sync**: Automatically extract and sync your role, goals, and tech stack.
*   **Vector Memory Engine**: High-performance RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) powered by ChromaDB.
- **Multi-Platform Support**: Deep integration with ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Copilot.
- **Neural Mindmap**: Visualizing your cognitive connections in real-time.
